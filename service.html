Services

Our Services

Health Care

From digital health record management, medication reminders, and home visits by doctors and nurses, we have the expertise to provide holistic home care services and elder care services.

Convenience

Support with daily chores, taking meds, and keeping company. Our dedicated community managers are experts at providing home care services.

Emergency Care

Technology-driven 24/7 specialized emergency elderly support backed by bike responders and elderly care paramedics to provide swift patient care services.

Safety

Home customization for the elderly to ensure safety from events such as slips and falls is a part of our home care services. The solution covers various sensors that trigger immediate responses.

Engagement

Organizes a variety of community events that provide an avenue for elders to live, learn, play, and earn. Our elder care services include concerted efforts to help the elderly earn an income such as through promoting their skill or talent.

Critical Nursing Care

A team of certified and trained attendants, nurses, physiotherapists, and doctors provide critical care at home. At-home critical care services are usually taken up post the patient has gone through ICU care in the hospital.

Doctor Visit

Elders with COPD,hypertension, acute diseases, heart diseases,alzheimer's , arthritis , emphysema , seasonal flu, and bed-bound patients can also benefit from our facility of such doctor at 24/7 visits..

Palliative Care

Palliative care is the effort of the doctors, nurses, attendants, and the patient's family to make the patient healthier and happier. The nurses stay beside the elder to take care of every need of the patient
